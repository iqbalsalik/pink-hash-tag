Rangtales Art is a creative studio and art collective that focuses on producing artwork and visual storytelling with a distinct Indian cultural perspective. The studio is known for blending traditional Indian art forms with modern design sensibilities, creating pieces that reflect both heritage and contemporary narratives.
The studio uses a variety of art styles, from digital art and illustration to more traditional forms like Madhubani, Pattachitra, and Warli painting. Their works often feature vibrant colors, intricate patterns, and symbolic elements from Indian traditions.
